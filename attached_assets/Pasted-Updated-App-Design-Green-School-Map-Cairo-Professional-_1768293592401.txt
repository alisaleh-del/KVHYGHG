Updated App Design – Green School Map (Cairo) – Professional Version

Description:

Design a professional, modern, and highly interactive mobile and web application called “Green School Map”.

The app visualizes pollution risks around a large number of schools and factories in Cairo, Egypt using advanced GIS maps.
Users can log in using email and phone, filter schools by risk and distance, toggle map layers, reset the map, and explore detailed analytics.

Target Users:
Students, teachers, parents, urban planners, environmental researchers.

Enhanced Features & Controls:

Filter schools by pollution risk level

Options: Low / Medium / High

Multi-select supported

Filter by distance from factories

Adjustable range slider (0–20 km)

Highlights only schools within the selected range

Toggle Map Layers:

Show / Hide schools (with varying sizes for more clarity if dense)

Show / Hide factories (use distinct icons for small, medium, large factories)

Optional layers: major roads, traffic congestion, waste disposal sites

Reset Map Button

Returns map to default view centered on Cairo

Resets zoom, panning, and applied filters

Enhanced Map Design:

Professional GIS style with smooth zoom and pan

Clustering of schools/factories when zoomed out for readability

Interactive pop-ups with school/factory details on click

Color-coded pollution levels with gradient scale for fine-grained visualization

Increased Data Points:

Hundreds of schools across all Cairo districts

Major factories and industrial zones included

Clear differentiation of high-density zones
